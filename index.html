My Meds

Medication Manager and Interaction Finder

When I worked in the medical field, I often had to transfer a patient's medication list into a charting system. The challenge I often faced was that patients honestly couldn't remember what medications they took. Some had more than twenty different medications. Most folks just had a plastic grocery bag of pill bottles for me to sift through.

This inspired me to make My Meds. This app lets you create a cloud-based medication list for you to access on your smartphone or computer. From there you can schedule what time you take each medication, get label information like warnings, indications, etc. As well as check if there are any dangerous interactions between the medications you take.

Cork Board

Bookmark Sharing and Organizing App

Corkboard is an app that allows users to create a place to store, categorize and share bookmarks. I don't know about you, but my bookmarks folder is huge and unwieldy. Wouldn't it be better if you could tag bookmarks instead of putting them in folders, and then be able to search by those tags?

Corkboard handles all of that for you. You can add tags, apply them to your bookmarks, and then search. Even better, you get a url for each new board you create so you can share it with your class, friends, or team. Now everyone can collaborate and share those useful links together in one place.

Go Fish Yourself

Multiplayer Online Card Game

Go Fish Yourself is an online multiplayer card game that runs off of google FIREBASE. It has a lobby system that automatically matches up players in pairs of two to play a game of go fish. The game has a chat function as well so you can hurl insults at each other like respectable adults would. There's even a chat command to automatically generate insults as you play!

I know what you're thinking. What if nobody is online to play with? Don't worry. I implemented a single-player mode to allow you to play against an AI opponent. You can even hurl insults at the computer if you want in the chat.
